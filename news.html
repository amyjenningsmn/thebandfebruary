Available while you wait...

// December 18, 2015 //

2016 is almost here which means the "Tomorrow Is Today" Re-Release is right around the snowy corner. Or slushy spring corner. Or sunny summer corner. Who knows is basically what we're getting at. But it'll be here in 2016! Fear not!

To satisfy the shoegaze fever dream you find yourself experiencing, might we suggest a couple compilations we've found ourselves on? The good people at St. Marie Records have put together some great comps over the years. Here are the two we've got tracks on:

The third annual compilation release by the sonically-driven label Saint Marie Records. Featuring tracks exclusively from the Saint Marie roster, it includes tracks from past and future releases, remixes, and exclusives.
This 3 CD set or MP3 download has something special for all you Slowdive fans: the third CD being exclusively covers of Slowdive songs spanning the band's entire career.
Included Feb Track: Souvenir.

Go get it.

The fourth annual compilation release by Saint Marie Records. Similar to Static Waves 3, this 3 CD set or MP3 download features artists exclusively on the St. Marie Roster. Bringing the sonic swirl!
Included Feb Track on this gem: Slan.

Go get this one too.

We'll keep you posted peeps.
