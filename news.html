Did you miss us 2?

// October 19, 2016 //

Hey y'all! Our record 'Tomorrow is Today' is being re-issued by Saint Marie Records in 2016! We're really stoked.

Here's the deal: we're not a band anymore but we're still pals. We won't be playing shows most likely unless something crazy happened and we were all in the same city and wanted to, which everyone knows is sort of possible, theoretically. But probably not.

What we are gonna do is kick 'Tomorrow is Today' back into this today, make this website, get our social media on, and have some fun.

We hope you like it.
